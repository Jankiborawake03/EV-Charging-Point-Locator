Powering the Future, Together

Building India's largest community-driven EV charging network

Our Mission

To revolutionize EV charging infrastructure by empowering homeowners to share their charging points, creating a sustainable and accessible network for all electric vehicle users.

We believe in a future where finding a charging station is as easy as finding a café – with the added benefit of supporting local communities.

How It Works

Register Your Charging Point

Homeowners can register their charging stations on our platform and set availability hours.

Find Local Charging

EV users can discover conveniently located charging points in their vicinity.

Charge & Pay

Users pay a fair rate directly to the hosts, creating a win-win situation for all.
